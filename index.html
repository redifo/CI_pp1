Popular Life Hacks

Tangled cords and cables can be frustrating and time-consuming to untangle.

Solution: Use bread clips (the plastic clips used to seal bread bags) to organize and label your cords. Here's how: Save bread clips from your bread bags. Attach a bread clip to each cord or cable. Write the name of the device or purpose on the bread clip using a marker. Bundle and store your cords neatly.
