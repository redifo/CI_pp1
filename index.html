Popular Life Hacks

Tangled cords and cables can be frustrating and time-consuming to untangle.

Solution: Use bread clips (the plastic clips used to seal bread bags) to organize and label your cords. Here's how: Save bread clips from your bread bags. Attach a bread clip to each cord or cable. Write the name of the device or purpose on the bread clip using a marker. Bundle and store your cords neatly.

Use 20 second rule to help making habits easier.

Make positive habits more accessible by reducing the time it takes to start them. For example, if you want to read more, place a book on your nightstand rather than on a distant shelf.

Prevent melting ice cubes ruining the taste of strong iced coffee

Freeze leftover coffee in ice cube trays. Use these coffee cubes instead of regular ice cubes to prevent dilution in your iced coffee.

Cleaning a Microwave can be made easier.

Place a lemon half in a bowl of water and microwave it for a couple of minutes. The steam will help loosen grime, and the lemon will leave a fresh scent.

Feeling unmotivated and afraid of failure? This might help.

Replace negative self-talk with positive affirmations. Instead of saying "I can't do this," say "I will do my best, and I am capable." Positive affirmations have been proven to improve performance.

Do not have pancake mix dispenser at home? Don't worry.

Transfer pancake batter to a Ziploc bag, snip off a corner, and use it as a mess-free dispenser for perfectly shaped pancakes.
